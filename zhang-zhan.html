公民記者張展：武漢疫情的真相記錄者 Citizen Journalist Zhang Zhan: Truth Teller of Wuhan

2020年初，當COVID-19疫情在武漢爆發時，前律師、公民記者張展逆行進入封城中的武漢。她用鏡頭和筆，記錄下了醫院的擁擠、社區的封鎖以及普通市民的恐懼與無助。

揭露真相的代價

張展發布了大量視頻，質疑當局的防疫措施，並試圖為受害者家屬發聲。這些真實的報導觸怒了當局。2020年5月，她在武漢被捕，隨後被押送回上海。

獄中的抗爭

2020年12月28日，上海浦東新區法院以“尋釁滋事罪”判處張展有期徒刑四年。在獄中，張展長期進行絕食抗議，以證明自己的清白。她的健康狀況一度極度惡化，體重降至不足40公斤，生命垂危。

持續的關注

儘管張展已於2024年5月刑滿釋放，但她依然受到嚴密的監控，行動自由受限。她的勇氣和犧牲，讓世界看到了中國公民記者在追求真相道路上所付出的巨大代價。

In early 2020, when the COVID-19 outbreak began in Wuhan, former lawyer and citizen journalist Zhang Zhan traveled into the locked-down city against the flow. With her camera and pen, she documented the crowded hospitals, community lockdowns, and the fear and helplessness of ordinary citizens.

The Price of Revealing the Truth

Zhang Zhan published numerous videos questioning the authorities' epidemic prevention measures and attempting to speak out for the families of victims. These truthful reports angered the authorities. In May 2020, she was arrested in Wuhan and subsequently transferred back to Shanghai.

Resistance in Prison

On December 28, 2020, the Shanghai Pudong New Area Court sentenced Zhang Zhan to four years in prison for "picking quarrels and provoking trouble." In prison, Zhang Zhan went on a prolonged hunger strike to prove her innocence. Her health deteriorated severely at one point, her weight dropping to less than 40 kg, leaving her in critical condition.

Continued Attention

Although Zhang Zhan was released in May 2024 after serving her sentence, she remains under tight surveillance with restricted freedom of movement. Her courage and sacrifice have shown the world the immense price Chinese citizen journalists pay in their pursuit of the truth.
