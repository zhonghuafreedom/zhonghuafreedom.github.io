廣州十二人案：偷渡海上的香港抗爭者 The Hong Kong 12: Protesters Caught at Sea

2020 年 8 月 23 日，12 名因 2019 年反送中運動被警方追訴的香港青年，乘坐快艇試圖橫越南海前往台灣尋求庇護。航行途中，他們在公海上被中國海警截獲，押回深圳鹽田看守所秘密羈押逾百日。最年輕者僅 16 歲。

逃亡的緣由

船上多人面臨「暴動罪」、「製造爆炸物罪」等重罪指控，最高可判 10 年以上。在香港國安法生效兩個月後，他們對司法已不抱信心，選擇走險路偷渡。其中李宇軒因協助黎智英《蘋果日報》眾籌國際倡議廣告，被視為國安重點目標。

家屬召開記者會與被打壓

被截獲後，家屬在香港聘請律師赴深圳爭取會見，全部被拒。當局指派「官派律師」取代家屬聘請的律師，多名家屬律師遭吊銷執照。家屬召開記者會時，多次被「親建制人士」騷擾。9 月 12 日，數百名國際法律界、政界人士聯署要求釋放他們。

深圳鹽田秘密審判

2020年12月30日，深圳鹽田法院對其中 10 名成年被告以「組織他人偷越邊境罪」判處 7 個月至 3 年不等。兩名未成年人遣返香港。所有判決均不公開、家屬與外國觀察員不得到場。

遣返後仍未得自由

2021 年陸續服刑期滿者被遣返香港，旋即被香港警方以原本案件再度逮捕，繼續審判。其中李宇軒等人後被併入「黎智英案」與「攬炒巴案」，至 2025 年仍在牢中。

結語：海，曾是最後的逃生通道

廣州十二人事件揭示，2020 年起，連大海也不再是港人的逃生通道。北京的長臂執法跨越領海，將國安法的恐懼直接延伸到太平洋。這群最年輕的逃亡者，是香港這一代「末日青年」的縮影——他們的勇氣，也是這座城市最後的尊嚴。

On August 23, 2020, twelve young Hongkongers facing prosecution for their roles in the 2019 anti-extradition movement boarded a speedboat across the South China Sea, hoping to reach Taiwan and claim asylum. En route, they were intercepted in international waters by China Coast Guard vessels and secretly detained at Yantian Detention Center in Shenzhen for more than 100 days. The youngest was only 16.

Why They Fled

Several faced charges of "rioting" or "manufacturing explosives," carrying possible sentences of over 10 years. With the National Security Law just two months old, they had lost faith in the courts and chose the dangerous sea route. Among them, Andy Li Yu-hin had helped Jimmy Lai's Apple Daily run international crowdfunded advocacy ads—making him a primary national security target.

Families Press Conferences and Suppression

After interception, families hired Hong Kong lawyers who travelled to Shenzhen seeking visits— all were refused. Authorities replaced family-retained counsel with state-appointed lawyers, and several family lawyers were later disbarred. Family press conferences were repeatedly harassed by "pro-establishment supporters." On September 12, hundreds of international legal and political figures issued an open letter demanding their release.

Secret Trial in Yantian, Shenzhen

On December 30, 2020, the Yantian District People's Court sentenced ten adult defendants to between 7 months and 3 years for "organizing illegal border crossings." Two minors were repatriated to Hong Kong. None of the proceedings were public; families and foreign observers were barred from attending.

No Freedom After Repatriation

In 2021, those completing their sentences were repatriated to Hong Kong and immediately re-arrested by HK police on the original underlying charges. Andy Li and others were later folded into the Jimmy Lai case and the "Lam Bun" case; as of 2025, several remain in custody.

Conclusion: Even the Sea Stopped Being a Way Out

The Hong Kong 12 case showed that from 2020 onward, even the open sea was no longer an escape route for Hongkongers. Beijing's long arm crossed territorial waters, projecting the fear of the NSL into the Pacific. These young runaways are the epitome of Hong Kong's "doomsday generation"—their courage is also the city's last dignity.
