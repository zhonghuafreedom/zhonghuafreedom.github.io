數字監控與隱私權的終結 Digital Surveillance and the End of Privacy

隨著面部識別和社會信用體系的全面推廣，普通公民的隱私空間正在被極度壓縮。中國正在建立一個前所未有的數字監控國家，每一個公民的一舉一動都在政府的掌控之中。

無處不在的面部識別

從地鐵站到商場，從居民小區到公共廁所，面部識別攝像頭無處不在。這些攝像頭不僅用於治安監控，更被用於追蹤異見人士、維權律師以及少數民族群體。個人的行蹤軌跡被實時上傳至公安數據庫。

社會信用體系：數字化的枷鎖

社會信用體系通過收集公民的金融、社交、法律等數據，對每個人進行評分。分數低的人將面臨出行限制（如無法購買高鐵票、機票）、貸款困難甚至子女入學受限等懲罰。這實際上是一種數字化的種姓制度。

數字足跡的全面監控

微信、支付寶等超級應用程序成為了政府監控公民的重要工具。聊天記錄、消費記錄、位置信息等隱私數據對政府完全透明。任何“不當”言論都可能導致賬號被封禁，甚至招致警察上門。

威權主義的技術輸出

令人擔憂的是，中國正在向其他威權國家出口這些監控技術，幫助它們建立類似的監控體系。這不僅威脅著中國人民的自由，也對全球的人權狀況構成了嚴重挑戰。

With the comprehensive promotion of facial recognition and the social credit system, the privacy of ordinary citizens is being severely compressed. China is building an unprecedented digital surveillance state where every move of every citizen is under government control.

Ubiquitous Facial Recognition

From subway stations to shopping malls, from residential compounds to public restrooms, facial recognition cameras are everywhere. These cameras are used not only for security monitoring but also for tracking dissidents, human rights lawyers, and ethnic minority groups. Individuals' movements are uploaded to public security databases in real-time.

Social Credit System: Digital Shackles

The social credit system scores everyone by collecting citizens' financial, social, and legal data. Those with low scores face punishments such as travel restrictions (inability to buy high-speed train or plane tickets), difficulty obtaining loans, and even restrictions on their children's education. This is effectively a digital caste system.

Total Surveillance of Digital Footprints

Super apps like WeChat and Alipay have become key tools for government surveillance of citizens. Chat logs, transaction records, and location data are completely transparent to the government. Any "inappropriate" speech can lead to account bans or even police visits.

Exporting Authoritarian Tech

Worryingly, China is exporting these surveillance technologies to other authoritarian regimes, helping them build similar surveillance systems. This threatens not only the freedom of the Chinese people but also poses a serious challenge to global human rights.
